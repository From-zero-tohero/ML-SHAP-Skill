# Figure and table set

## Core prediction figures

1. Workflow diagram when needed.
2. Target distribution and/or time–space evolution.
3. Correlation matrix after noting that correlation is not causation.
4. Cross-validation and holdout model comparison.
5. Regression: observed versus predicted and out-of-time series.
6. Regression: residual diagnostics.
7. Classification: confusion matrix, ROC, precision–recall, and calibration.

## Core explanation figures

1. Mean absolute SHAP bar chart.
2. SHAP beeswarm / 蜂群图（蜂巢图） for feature importance, contribution direction, feature-value distribution, and sample-level heterogeneity.
3. Dependence plots for the most important or theory-relevant variables.
4. Interaction heatmap and selected two-variable interaction plots.
5. Waterfall plots for representative and failure cases.
6. Grouped or temporal SHAP plots when the design has regimes or subgroups.

## Core tables

1. Variable definitions, units, roles, and sources.
2. Data-quality audit and descriptive statistics.
3. Split and validation design.
4. Model families and hyperparameter search space.
5. Cross-validation and untouched-test metrics.
6. Robustness and sensitivity results.
7. SHAP importance ranking and direction summary.
8. Nonlinear shapes, candidate thresholds, uncertainty, and support range.
9. Interaction ranking.
10. Subgroup performance and explanations.

Move secondary diagnostics and exhaustive tuning results to supplementary outputs.
